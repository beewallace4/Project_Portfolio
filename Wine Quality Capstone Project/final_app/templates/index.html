Wine Quality Analysis with Machine Learning and Tableau

This project uses machine learning techniques to predict the quality of wine based on several features such as alcohol content, pH value, acidity, residual sugar, and more. We produced Tableau dashboards to visualize our analysis, and an interactive Machine Learning app for you to determine the quality of your own wine, right here on the website. We drew inspiration from our love for data and wine. We used publicly available datasets of red and white wines, each with its own set of features and a quality score.

Our predictive classification model can (somewhat) accurately classify wines into two quality categories while also classifying whether the wine is Red or White. An additional goal of ours was to find the region where the best rated wine originated and where the most highly-rated wines are produced. To represent this we used Tableau visualizations of bar charts, graphs, maps, and more. We hope you enjoy our work as much as you enjoy a glass of Vino. Cheers!
